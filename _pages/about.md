I am currently a Ph.D. student at Hunan University, where my research focuses on spatial perception, visual grounding, and robotic manipulation.

I received my undergraduate education in Automation from North China Electric Power University in 2021. During my undergraduate studies, I worked on low-level robotic systems, including motion control algorithms, hardware development, and FreeRTOS-based embedded system design.

My current research investigates the mismatch between observer-centered language descriptions and robot-arm egocentric perception caused by inconsistent reference frames. In particular, I am interested in how robots can correctly understand spatial descriptions, localize referred objects, and apply this ability to robotic grasping and manipulation tasks.

Research Interests
======

- Robotic manipulation and grasping
- Spatial perception and visual grounding
- Reference-frame-aware language understanding
- Vision-language models for robotics
- Embodied AI and robot perception
